Our Mission

Guiding Students Toward a Business Future

We believe every student deserves access to real business education — not just theory, but hands-on experiences, inspiring panels, and connections that open doors.

Founded to bridge the gap between classroom learning and the business world, YBA gives students the clarity, confidence, and community they need to succeed.
