FAQ

Questions students, families, and partners often ask.

This page helps visitors understand who YBA serves, how membership works, and what to expect from programs and events.

Who is YBA for?

YBA is designed for students who want to explore business, entrepreneurship, leadership, and professional growth. If you serve a specific age range or grade band, this is a good place to state it clearly.

Does joining cost anything?

No. Membership and student participation are described throughout the site as free, including on Join. Keep that promise consistent everywhere.

Do students need prior business experience?

No. YBA is beginner-friendly and meant to help students build confidence whether they are brand new to business topics or already pursuing an idea.

What kinds of events does YBA host?

YBA hosts workshops, speaker panels, networking events, and project-based learning opportunities focused on practical business skills and career exposure.

Where do events take place?

Events may be virtual, in schools, at community spaces, or through partner locations. If your programming is centered in a city or district, add that here so families and schools know where you operate.

How do students join?

Students can use the email signup form on the Join page. After submitting, they should receive a welcome email when the EmailJS setup is fully configured.

Can schools or organizations partner with YBA?

Yes. Schools, youth programs, businesses, and community groups can collaborate through co-hosted events, student referrals, sponsorships, mentorship, or volunteer support.

How can adults support the nonprofit?

Professionals and community members can mentor students, speak at events, volunteer, sponsor programs, or open doors to useful partnerships. See Get Involved for details.
