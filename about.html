About Youth Business Association

Building access, confidence, and real-world business readiness.

YBA exists to help students move from curiosity to capability through mentorship, practical learning, and community.

Who We Are

A nonprofit designed to connect students with opportunity.

We bridge the space between classroom learning and the professional world so students can explore business in a way that feels real, relevant, and empowering.

Our programs are intentionally practical. Students meet professionals, ask honest questions, build foundational knowledge, and practice the kinds of communication and problem-solving skills that matter in college, careers, and entrepreneurship.

What Students Gain

Career clarity

Students hear directly from professionals and start to understand which paths fit their interests and strengths.

Confidence

Community

YBA creates a place where driven students can meet others who care about leadership, innovation, and impact.
